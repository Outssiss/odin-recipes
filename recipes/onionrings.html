Onion Rings

Description

Deep fried ring shaped onions covered in a batter mix of flour, salt and water.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Flour
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix 50/50 water and flour in a bowl
  2. Add a teaspoon of salt to the mix
  3. Cut the onion in rings
  4. Dip the rings in the batter
  5. Deep fry the batter covered rings until golden brown
