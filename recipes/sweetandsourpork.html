Sweet and Sour Pork

Description

Sweet and sour pork sits among some of the most well-known Chinese dishes outside of China.

It's widely believed to be a Cantonese dish, known colloquially as gú lóuh yuhk, which roughly translates to “ancient pork”.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Cornstarch
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Onion
