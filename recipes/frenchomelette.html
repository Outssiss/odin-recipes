French Omelette

Description

Folded omelette juicy on the inside.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs on a bowl
  2. Add salt and pepper
  3. Mix
  4. Cook on a pan in high-medium heat while mixing
  5. When there's almost no liquid eggs left fold the omelette in half
  6. Serve
